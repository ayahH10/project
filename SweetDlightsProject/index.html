Matilda Cake

A rich, dense chocolate cake inspired by the movie Matilda.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Preparation:

Mix all ingredients and bake for 35 minutes at 180°C. Let it cool and cover with chocolate ganache.

Macaron

A light, delicate French pastry with a creamy filling.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1½ cups powdered sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • ½ cup sugar

Preparation:

Whisk egg whites, fold in dry ingredients, pipe into circles, and bake for 15 minutes. Fill with cream.

Croissant

A buttery, flaky pastry with a crescent shape.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp yeast

Preparation:

Layer dough with butter, fold and roll several times. Shape into crescents and bake at 200°C for 20 minutes.
